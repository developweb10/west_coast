Pro Shop

We stock a wide variety of firearms, ammunition, and accessories to assist in your range session or next class. We’ve got you covered for magazines, optics, cleaning supplies and so much more. If there is something we don’t have on hand, we can order it especially for you. In addition to firearms, we are also a Class III dealer which means we can help you navigate the world of suppressors; it can be a doozy!

Purchasing a Firearm

Not everyone needs a tricked-out competition gun nor is there one firearm that fits everyone. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is trained to help you determine the right firearm for your needs. All you will need to do is bring your Washington State Photo ID with your FULL LEGAL NAME and CURRENT RESIDENTIAL ADDRESS. Permanent Residents and Non-Immigrant Visa Holders will need to bring a few other documents.

NFA / CLASS III

We’re partnered with Silencer Shop to make the suppressor buying process as easy as, well, as easy as filling out an ATF form. Hey, at least you won’t pay any transfer fees when buying through Silencer Shop!

TRANSFERS

We facilitate online purchases and private face-to-face transfers. But we can often save you money by ordering the firearm for you directly. Check with us for availability and pricing before you buy online to make sure you’re getting the best deal possible. Stop in or call (425) 265-7143.
