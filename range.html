West Coast Armory North Indoor Shooting Range

At the West Coast Armory North Indoor Shooting Range, you'll find helpful staff that wants to make your experience here safe, fun, and educational. Whether you're new to firearms, in the market to buy a new firearm (but not sure which one is right for you), or just want to keep up your skill level, we welcome you to our range!

Basic Firearm Safety Rules

  • Always treat all firearms as loaded.
  • Never point a firearm at anything you are not willing to shoot.
  • Keep your finger off the trigger unless you are on target and have made the decision to shoot.
  • Be sure of your target and what is behind and beyond your target.
  • All firearms must be cased or holstered when entering the range. You may uncase/unholster in the firing booth.
  • No uncased/unholstered guns outside the firing booth. You may load your magazines at the back table.
  • No steel or aluminum cased ammunition.
  • No lead tip ammunition, except for .22lr.
  • No birdshot, game load, slugs, or target load shotgun rounds allowed.
  • No food/drinks in the range (you may enjoy them in the waiting area).

Rules for renting Firearms

For Liability and Safety purposes, West Coast Armory North will not allow you to rent a firearm unless you meet at least one of the following requirements:

  1. You bring your own personal firearm(s) with you on your visit to our range.
  2. You bring a friend or family member with you on your visit to our range.

These precautions are taken as an effort to prevent any dangerous shooters in our range, and to discourage the use of our firearms for your own personal use.

If you would like to try out a variety of firearms, but do not own one yet, and/or are unable to persuade a friend or relative to accompany you to the range, we recommend that you take an introductory pistol course.

Check our calendar for the training schedule.
